General

Lagazuoi S.p.A. (referred to as "Lagazuoi" or "we") places great importance on the careful handling of personal data. We are committed to processing and securing personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy regulations.

Personal Data we process

Lagazuoi may process various types of personal data necessary for providing our services. This can include direct identification details such as names, contact details like email addresses, and personal characteristics such as age categories. For a detailed explanation of the cookies we use, please refer to our cookie policy.

Purpose of personal data processing

Lagazuoi collects, uses, and manages the personal data provided by you or collected by Lagazuoi for all purposes necessary to provide our services and ensure the proper functioning of our website(s). Data deletion

If you wish to delete your personal data from the App, you can do so by deleting the caches and related data stored on the device. Disclosure to third parties

Your personal data will not be sold, transferred, or otherwise disclosed to third parties without your explicit consent, unless it is necessary for the purposes stated in our data protection policy or as required by law.

In such cases, we will ensure that the transfer complies with applicable legal obligations and that appropriate measures are in place to protect your personal data.
